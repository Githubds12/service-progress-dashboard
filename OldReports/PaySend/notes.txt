PaySend Analysis Notes:
- App uses a mix of JSON and XML.
- Registration starts with a JSON POST to /api/json/registration.
- Headers are critical: Client-Software, sec_uuid, X-Phone-Country, and auth_type: ANONYMOUS.
- Response provides balId and authKey.
- OTP submission uses XML POST to /api/xml.jsp with request_type: submit_smsconfirm_code.
- No Captcha or payload encryption observed in the flow.
- Automation is straightforward if headers are correctly set.
